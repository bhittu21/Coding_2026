About the Role

We're seeking a creative and motivated Graphics Design Intern to join our team for a 2-month paid internship. You'll work on real client projects, creating scroll-stopping social media graphics for hotels, restaurants, and lifestyle brands. This is a hands-on opportunity to build your portfolio, learn industry best practices, and grow your design skills in a fast-paced creative environment.

Required Skills

Canva (Advanced)
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Typography
Color Theory
Social Media Design

What You'll Do

  • Create stunning Instagram posts, stories, reels, and carousel designs for client brands
  • Design promotional graphics, menu cards, and digital marketing materials
  • Collaborate with the team to develop creative concepts and visual strategies
  • Maintain brand consistency across all design deliverables
  • Participate in brainstorming sessions and contribute creative ideas
  • Revise and refine designs based on feedback from clients and team members
  • Stay updated with the latest design trends and social media best practices

What We're Looking For

  • Strong portfolio demonstrating creative design work (required)
  • Proficiency in Canva, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator
  • Understanding of typography, color theory, and composition
  • Passion for social media and current design trends
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work in a team
  • Self-motivated with a willingness to learn and grow
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Design or related field (preferred)

What You'll Get

  • Paid internship with competitive compensation
  • Hands-on experience working on real client projects
  • Mentorship from experienced designers
  • Portfolio-building opportunities with premium brands
  • Flexible working hours and remote-friendly environment
  • Certificate of completion at the end of the internship
  • Potential for full-time employment based on performance
