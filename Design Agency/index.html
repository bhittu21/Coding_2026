About

Built for social-first brands

aolo.design is a small studio focused on visuals that feel premium but ship fast: perfect for hotels, cafes, and lifestyle brands launching in Sylhet and beyond.

The goal is simple: make your social feeds look like a high-end landing page—clean hierarchy, depth, and motion-inspired layouts that stop the scroll.

Prototype process

  • 1. Quick call to understand your brand, menu, or offer.
  • 2. 9-grid or story sequence concept designed within 72 hours.
  • 3. Revisions, then a full template pack for the month.

As you grow, we can layer in basic campaign planning and boosted posts while keeping the design system consistent.
